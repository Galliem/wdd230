Sport Tryouts

Poolville is hosting tryouts for the many of the school sports teams. Go to their website or ask your students for more information.

Farmer's Market

Poolville is hosting a local farmer's market this summer! Join us at the park to see what our farms have been working on this season. There will be plenty of room for the kids to run and play while the rest of the family can mingle amongst the neighbors. Mark your calander now!
